Tennis at St Hilda's

Highlights


Competitions:

There are 50 who girls play in the Secondary Schools competition in terms 1 and 4.

There are 6 year 7/8's who play in the Primary competition.

There is a Premier Tennis Team of six of our best players who play in various competitions, these include:

  • Quad against St Margaret's, Rangi and Craighead.
  • Secondary Schools Otago Champs.
  • Otago Championships.

There is also a competitive school competition held in term 4 to determine the senior and junior tennis champions. These players recieve trophies.
