Tennis at St Hilda's

Home


Tennis for year 9-13 St Hilda's students is played during terms 1 and 4 at the Logan Park Tennis Courts. No experience is required, you just need a racket, and a team of 2-3 people. Many girls at St Hilda's, mostly those from year 9-12, (9+10 especially), choose to play tennis over the summer.
